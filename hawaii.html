Hawaii

Volcanic Wonders, Emerald Valleys, and Aloha Spirit

Overview

An isolated archipelago in the Central Pacific, Hawaii is a paradise defined by its dramatic volcanic landscapes, world-class surfing, and rich Polynesian heritage. From the red-hot lava of the Big Island to the lush Na Pali Coast, it is a destination of pure natural energy.

Best Time March — September
Currency US Dollar ($)
Language English & Hawaiian

Must Visit

  • Waikiki Beach: The iconic heart of Honolulu, perfect for beginners' surfing and sunset cocktails.
  • Na Pali Coast: Breathtaking emerald cliffs on Kauai accessible only by boat, helicopter, or hiking.
  • Haleakala National Park: A massive dormant volcano on Maui offering surreal sunrise views above the clouds.
  • Pearl Harbor: A historic memorial site on Oahu honoring the legacy of World War II.

The Taste of Hawaii

Experience a traditional Lūʻau, try a fresh Ahi Poké Bowl, and refresh yourself with a massive Shave Ice topped with tropical fruit syrups and condensed milk.
