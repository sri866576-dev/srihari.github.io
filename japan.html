Japan

Ancient Temples, Neon Cities, and Timeless Traditions

Overview

Japan is a mesmerizing archipelago where the deep past meets the distant future. From the serene Zen gardens of Kyoto to the bustling, neon-lit streets of Tokyo, it offers an unparalleled experience of precision, hospitality, and natural beauty.

Best Time March — May
Currency Japanese Yen (¥)
Language Japanese

Must Visit

  • Kyoto: The cultural heart of Japan, home to thousands of stunning temples and traditional geisha districts.
  • Tokyo: A high-tech metropolis famous for its world-class shopping, dining, and the iconic Shibuya Crossing.
  • Mount Fuji: Japan’s most sacred peak, offering breathtaking views and incredible hiking opportunities.
  • Nara: A peaceful city where friendly deer roam freely through historic parklands and ancient shrines.

The Taste of Japan

Savor authentic Sushi at a Tsukiji market, warm up with a bowl of Hakata Ramen, and enjoy Matcha Green Tea paired with traditional Wagashi sweets.
