Spain

Elegance, History, and Sun-drenched Coasts

Overview

From the rugged cliffs of the Atlantic coast to the Mediterranean coves, Spain is a tapestry of diverse cultures and landscapes. Known for its laid-back lifestyle, world-class art, and legendary gastronomy.

Best Time April — June
Currency Euro (€)
Language Spanish

Must Visit

  • Seville: The heart of Andalusia, famous for Flamenco and the Plaza de España.
  • Barcelona: Home to Gaudí’s architectural masterpieces and urban beaches.
  • Madrid: The golden triangle of art museums and vibrant nightlife.
  • Granada: Where the stunning Alhambra palace meets snowy Sierra Nevada peaks.

The Taste of Spain

Don't leave without trying Paella Valenciana, fresh Tapas in San Sebastián, and Churros con Chocolate for breakfast.
