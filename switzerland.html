Switzerland

Alpine Peaks, Crystal Lakes, and Timeless Elegance

Overview

Nestled in the heart of the Alps, Switzerland is a masterclass in natural beauty and precision. From world-class ski resorts to peaceful lakeside cities, it offers a serene escape defined by breathtaking vistas and high-end luxury.

Best Time June — August
Currency Swiss Franc (CHF)
Language German, French, Italian

Must Visit

  • Zermatt: A car-free mountain village at the base of the iconic, pyramid-shaped Matterhorn.
  • Lucerne: Known for its preserved medieval architecture and the stunning Lake Lucerne.
  • Interlaken: Nestled between two lakes, it is the ultimate hub for outdoor adventure and paragliding.
  • Lauterbrunnen: A fairytale valley featuring 72 cascading waterfalls and vertical limestone cliffs.

The Taste of Switzerland

Indulge in authentic Cheese Fondue in the mountains, try the potato-based Rösti, and don't miss world-famous Swiss Chocolate from local chocolatiers.
